Strength & Conditioning

This program focuses on strength and conditioning, which helps in improving a skill or performance in a sport. It strengthens core stability and improves speed, endurance, and athletic performance. In, fact, it focuses upon the physical and physiological development of athletes for elite sports performance. The strength and conditioning program uses exercise prescription specifically for the improvement of performance in athletic competition and thus can be done by all age groups including teens and athletes.

Inhale. Exhale.

The benefits that your body can reap from yoga are multifold; contrary to popular belief it does much more than just increase your flexibility. Yoga also increases muscle strength and tone, improves respiration, heart health, circulatory health, athletic performance and energy and vitality. The way we teach yoga at Multifit, we go beyond all of this and work on improving your will-power and make you fit both physically and mentally.

Mix Martial Arts

MMA (Mix Martial Arts) is a full body workout that pushes your body to the limit like no other sport while working your upper body, lower body and core. It is also a high intensity workout that challenges your cardiovascular muscles and burns calories quickly. You can burn up to 590 calories in a single 45-minute session.
